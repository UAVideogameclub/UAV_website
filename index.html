Who are we?

The University of Auckland Video Game Club are an inclusive and expanding club, composed of gamers attending the University of Auckland. If you've ever found yourself wanting to play Smash against someone other than your little brother, if you've ever tried to debate the better skill build for a hero, if you've ever wanted to be a part of the diverse gaming community in Auckland or the University, then this club is for you!

Take a closer look at the club via Facebook, or our Steam group.

What do we do?

We host LAN parties throughout the year, we throw gaming competitions, we give out prizes and provide our members with discounts to stores such as JB Hi-Fi and PB Tech, and we also shout our members pizza on occasion. We hook our members up with exclusive events and deals, Sound good? For five dollars, you gain this much and more for the whole year - click here to sign up.
