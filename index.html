Who are we?

The University of Auckland Video Game Club are an inclusive and expanding club, focused on video games, composed of gamers attending the University of Auckland. If you've ever found yourself wanting to play Smash against someone other than your little brother, if you've ever debated a better skill build for a hero, if you've ever wanted to be a part of the diverse gaming community in Auckland or the University, then this club is for you!

Scroll onwards to find out how to join the UAV, or take a closer look at the club via Facebook, or our Steam group. Also, don't hesitate to contact us either if you need more information.

What do we do?

We host LAN parties throughout the year, we throw gaming competitions, we give out prizes and provide our members with discounts to stores such as JB Hi-Fi and PB Tech, and we also shout our members pizza on occasion. We hook our members up with exclusive events and deals - last year we ran several competitions, including Dota 2, CS:GO, Hearthstone, and League of Legends events, and collaborating with the University of Auckland Business School, as well as other New Zealand universities such as Otago and Victoria University. Sound good? For ten dollars, you gain this much and more for the whole year - click here to sign up.

What benefits will I get?

As a member of the UAV, you will have access to all of our club events without payment. If numbers at events prove to be larger than we anticipate, you will be given priority access. You will have priority sign-ups for leagues and competitions. You also get to be part of this great community of gamers, who you can then personally own or be owned by at games of your choosing. Good luck ; )
Our members also receive discounts when buying from PB Tech at the University and CBD, as well as Playtech and JB Hi-Fi on Queen Street. More details will be announced soon. Watch this space!

How do I sign up?

To successfully become a member of the UAV, please fill out the form located here. A membership fee of $5 per semester must then be paid. Get in touch with one of the Executive Members, either online or at one of our club events, who will help you with payment options. Once payment has been confirmed, you will be granted an ID Card Sticker for the UAV, which officially designates you as one of us!
